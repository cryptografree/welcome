# Welcome to CryptograFREE

This github account and repositories are focused in the **CryptograFREE** project.

The main purpose of the project is protecting ***Fundamental Digital Rights***. In the current era, we find ourselves in a permanent dichotomy: the constant struggle to preserve privacy and the right to anonymity in the face of hyperconnectivity.

Governments and Corporations, the Elites, are always pushing the line to take advantage over the citizen: this is their nature and no one may get surprised. They are not the problem.

That is not the issue. The real problem arises when we (you, me), as CITIZENS, forget this truth and grow complacent—when we fail to do what is necessary to ensure we maintain the Rights guaranteed to us by our Constitutions.

We ARE NOT extremists, nor anti-system, nor <whatever>: we are citizens, we respect and defend the Law with the focus on RIGHTS. Law is not only about duties, but about Rights. When we forget that every duty we accept carries an associated right, Elites take control on you.
